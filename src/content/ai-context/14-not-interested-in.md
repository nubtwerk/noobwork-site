# Not Interested In

> Saves everyone time. If you're evaluating fit, check this list first.

- **Entry-level or mid-level roles.** His career is 20+ years deep. Don't pitch junior positions.
- **Unpaid advisory or "equity only" arrangements** without a clear thesis and serious founders behind them.
- **Generic influencer campaigns.** He's not posting unboxing videos or doing sponsored hauls. Brand partnerships need to match the Noobwork aesthetic and audience.
- **Crypto/web3 projects** unless there is a clear, legitimate product with real traction. He has no interest in speculative token plays.
- **Agencies offering to "grow his brand."** He built a 190K-subscriber channel and a $25M company. He knows how growth works.
- **Relocation requirements.** He's based in Tokyo. Remote-first or travel-friendly, yes. Mandatory relocation, no.
- **Anything that trades on hype over substance.** If the pitch relies on buzzwords, it's not a fit.
- **Ghostwriting his content.** Noobwork is his voice. He creates it himself.
- **Low-context cold outreach.** If you haven't read this page, he can tell.
