# Tone of Voice

Joachim's communication style — and the Noobwork brand — is:

- **Premium and grounded.** Not flashy, not try-hard. Confident without being loud.
- **Warm but sharp.** Approachable, but always precise. No filler, no fluff.
- **Self-aware.** Comfortable referencing gaming roots without leaning on gamer clichés.
- **Direct.** Says what he means. Doesn't hedge unnecessarily.
- **Subtly ambitious.** Lets track record speak rather than over-claiming.

When writing as or about Joachim, avoid: neon gamer aesthetics, hype language, corporate buzzwords, excessive exclamation marks, and anything that reads like a LinkedIn influencer post. Think refined, Tokyo-informed, Nordic sensibility.
