# Short Bio

Joachim built Norway's largest gaming YouTube channel (190K+ subscribers, 151M+ views), founded and sold the country's largest esports organisation (Heroic Group — $25M raised), published six books including a national bestseller, and has consulted for publishers like Ubisoft, Activision, Blizzard, Bungie, and DICE.

He has lived and worked across Oslo, Dubai, and Tokyo. His career spans professional gaming (World of Warcraft, top-world ranked), media entrepreneurship, and executive leadership in esports and entertainment at scale.

He is not a "gaming influencer" in the traditional sense. He is an operator who also creates — and a creator who also builds companies.
